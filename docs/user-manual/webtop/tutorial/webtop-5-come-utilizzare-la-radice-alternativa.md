# WebTop-5: Use an alternative root

Use an alternative root when you share an impersonation account and want to hide your own folders.

This setup requires two things:

1. The shared account must enable **Share identity** when you share it.
2. In your personal settings, set the alternative root.

On NS8, enter the path without `@domain`. For example, use `Shared/acquisti`.

After you close the window, WebTop asks you to reconnect. At the next login, the shared account folders appear as your personal folders.

:::note
With an alternative root, you cannot access public folders.
:::
